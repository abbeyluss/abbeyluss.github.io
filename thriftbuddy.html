A Community For Thrifters...

A place for thrifters to post and find clothing at their favorite thrifting spots!

About

Thrift Buddy is a website for the Boston thrifting community to post things they find while thrifting that don’t fit them or won’t work with their wardrobe, but they think others might enjoy. Posts are not a guarantee that an item will be available at a thrift store. However, they can give thrifters an idea of what pieces to look out for, or help them decide which thrift store to check out!

FAQ

How do I create a post?
Choose the thrift store you are currently at, and hit the “Make New Post” Button. Fill out the information and provide a picture!

What do I do if I buy an item that was posted?
Click on the post and hit “Remove Post” and for the reason click “I bought this!”.

What do I do if I can’t find the item at the thrift store?
Click on the post and hit “Remove Post” and for the reason click “I can’t find it!”
